# Methodology: EAA 2025 & WCAG 2.1 Compliance

Starting **June 28, 2025**, the European Accessibility Act (EAA) makes accessibility mandatory for e-commerce. Non-compliance can lead to heavy fines.

## 🏗️ Semantic AST Auditing

Syphio audits the "intent" of the code, not just the result.

### 1. Interactive Elements Logic

We check if `button` or `a` tags generated by Liquid have dynamic `aria-` attributes that change state.

- **Rule:** A Drawer (Cart) must have `aria-expanded` linked to its Liquid logic state.

### 2. Keyboard Navigation & Focus Traps

Detecting if modals or search overlays "trap" the user without a clear `ESC` key handling in the snippet logic.

## 🌍 Legal Impact

Syphio maps rules directly to **WCAG 2.1 AA** standards to ensure 100% legal coverage for EU Merchants.
